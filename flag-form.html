Flag Order Form


For your order to be processed, a check must be made payable to the State of Michigan. Send your check to:

Executive Office of the Governor
Attn: Flag Order Processing
PO Box 30013
Lansing MI 48909

Once the Executive Office of the Governor receives and processes the payment, you should receive your flag within (10) business days.

Information about your flag:

The flags are 3'x5' and are made of nylon. There is a certificate issued with the flag that certifies the flag was flown over the Michigan State Capitol. You may request a flag that was flown on a specific date, but it is based on availability and cannot be guaranteed.
